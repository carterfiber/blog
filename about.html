About Me

I picked up a camera just over a year ago to help my wife with her marketing project. It was my "I can do this" attitude that lead me to believe I could help in the first place. Long story short...I fell COMPLETELY in love with photography! Now I shoot stock photography, construction and events. My style tends to lean towards modern, yet classic, lifestyle images that transcends time.

I mostly shoot in Atlanta but will travel. Just message me and let's shoot.
